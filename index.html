EDUCATIONAL LEVEL IN NIGERIA

As a primary school student, you are expected to: Attend School: Regularly attend and be on time for all classes. Participate: Engage actively in classroom activities and discussions. Complete Homework: Finish and turn in homework assignments on time. Study: Review and practice what you've learned in school.

Additionally, focus on: Personal Development: Learn basic skills like reading, writing, math, and social skills. Self-Care: Maintain good hygiene, eat healthily, and get enough sleep. Respect: Show respect for teachers, classmates, and school rules. Cooperation: Work well with others in group activities and play. Curiosity: Be curious and eager to learn new things.

These expectations help students build a strong foundation for future learning and personal growth.
