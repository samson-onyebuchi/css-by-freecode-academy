PRIMARY

As a primary school student, you are expected to: Attend SchoolRegularly attend and be on time for all classes. Participate:Engage actively in classroom activities and discussions. Complete Homework:Finish and turn in homework assignments on time. Study:Review and practice what you've learned in school. Additionally, focus on: Personal Development:Learn basic skills like reading, writing, math, and social skills. Self-Care:Maintain good hygiene, eat healthily, and get enough sleep. Respect:Show respect for teachers, classmates, and school rules. Cooperation:Work well with others in group activities and play. Curiosity:Be curious and eager to learn new things. These expectations help students build a strong foundation for future learning and personal growth.

SECONDARY

As a secondary school student, you are expected to: Attend Classes:Regularly attend and be punctual for all classes. Engage:Actively participate in lessons, discussions, and group activities. Complete Assignments:Submit homework and projects on time, ensuring they meet required standards. Study:Dedicate time to understand and review course material. Maintain Integrity:Uphold principles of honesty, avoiding cheating and plagiarism. Additionally, focus on: Personal Growth:Develop good study habits, time management, and self-discipline. Self-Care:Maintain physical and mental health through balanced activities. Community Involvement:Participate in school activities, clubs, and sports, showing respect and inclusivity towards others. Skill Development:Build skills in communication, teamwork, and leadership. Future Planning:Start considering future educational and career paths, exploring interests and options. These expectations aim to help students succeed academically, develop personally, and prepare for future endeavors.

UNIVERSITY

As a university student, you are expected to: Attend Classes:Regularly attend and participate in lectures and seminars. Engage:Contribute to discussions and group work, and actively participate in learning. Complete Assignments:Submit coursework on time, adhering to academic standards. Study:Dedicate time to understand course material thoroughly. Maintain Integrity:Uphold academic honesty and avoid plagiarism. Additionally, focus on: Personal Growth:Develop critical thinking, time management, and resilience. Self-Care:Maintain physical and mental well-being. Community Engagement:Participate in campus activities, show respect for diversity, and foster a positive environment. Career Prep:Gain relevant skills, seek internships, and plan for your career. Lifelong Learning:Stay curious, adaptable, and open to new ideas, continually seeking improvement.
